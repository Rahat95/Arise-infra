Our 3-Step Quality Testing Process

Every batch undergoes rigorous laboratory analysis to ensure compliance with IS standards and optimal performance characteristics.

1

Silt Content Test

Precise measurement of silt and clay particles using sedimentation analysis. We maintain silt content below 3% for aggregates and under 8% for sand, ensuring optimal cement bonding and preventing long-term structural weakness.

Standard:
IS 2386 Part II
Tolerance:
±0.5% accuracy
Frequency:
Every batch
Method:
Jar sedimentation
2

Sieve Analysis

Comprehensive particle size distribution analysis using standardized mesh sieves. This ensures proper gradation for optimal packing density, workability, and strength development in concrete mixes.

Standard:
IS 2386 Part I
Mesh Sizes:
20mm to 75μm
Parameters:
Fineness modulus
Compliance:
Zone II/III grading
3

Moisture Content Check

Accurate moisture determination using oven-dry method to ensure proper mix design calculations. Controlled moisture levels prevent segregation during transport and enable precise water-cement ratio control.

Method:
Oven drying at 105°C
Accuracy:
±0.1% precision
Duration:
24-hour cycle
Range:
0.5% - 6% typical
