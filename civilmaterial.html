Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our materials, pricing, and delivery processes

What are your current MMR rates for aggregates and sand?

Our rates are updated daily based on direct-from-source pricing. Contact us for instant MMR rates as prices fluctuate based on market conditions and bulk order quantities. We guarantee the most competitive rates in Mumbai and Thane region.

What is your minimum order quantity for bulk orders?

We cater to large-scale projects with minimum orders starting from 100 cubic meters for aggregates and 50 cubic meters for M-Sand and River Sand. Larger quantities receive better pricing tiers.

How do you ensure quality standards for your materials?

All our materials undergo rigorous quality testing at source. We provide IS code compliance certificates, gradation reports, and material test certificates with every delivery. Our M-Sand meets IS 383:2016 standards.

What are your delivery timelines for Mumbai and Thane?

Standard delivery within 24-48 hours for confirmed orders. Emergency deliveries available within 12 hours for ongoing projects. We maintain a fleet of tipper trucks for reliable, on-time delivery across all major construction zones.

Do you offer credit terms for established contractors?

Yes, we provide flexible payment terms for verified contractors and construction companies. Credit facilities available with proper documentation and project verification. Contact our sales team for credit approval process.

Can you supply materials to remote construction sites?

We deliver to all accessible locations within Mumbai Metropolitan Region and Thane district. Additional transportation charges may apply for sites beyond our standard delivery zones. Site accessibility assessment provided free of charge.
