Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our materials, delivery, and ordering process.

We supply high-quality crushed stone aggregates (10mm, 20mm, 40mm), manufactured sand (M-sand), natural river sand, and stone dust. All materials are sourced from certified quarries and meet IS 383:2016 standards for concrete construction.

We deliver across Mumbai, Navi Mumbai, and Thane. Our delivery network covers all major construction sites within a 50km radius of our supply yards. Same-day delivery is available for orders placed before 12 PM.

Every batch undergoes rigorous testing in our certified laboratory for gradation, specific gravity, water absorption, and crushing strength. We maintain detailed quality certificates and provide test reports with each delivery upon request.

Minimum order quantity is 1 truck load (approximately 10-12 cubic meters). For smaller quantities, we can arrange consolidated deliveries. Bulk orders above 100 cubic meters qualify for special pricing and priority scheduling.
